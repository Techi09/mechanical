SHEET 02
01 — Definition

What is mechanical engineering?

A diverse, fundamental discipline that blends physics, mathematics, and material science to design and analyse physical systems. Mechanical engineers design, manufacture, test, and maintain machines — turning an idea on paper into functioning, real-world technology.

02 — Industries

Where mechanical engineers work

Every industry that builds, moves, or powers something needs a mechanical engineer on the drawing board.
